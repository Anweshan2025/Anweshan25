About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan — where imagination meets innovation.
Our Annual Science Exhibition – Anweshan 2025 – is an exciting platform for you to showcase your curiosity, creativity, and understanding of scientific concepts. Let your imagination take shape through models, demonstrations, and experiments that reflect the true spirit of science.
Please read the instructions carefully before filling out the Google Form. Each group must consist of 4 students. Mixed groups allowed for Std. 6 & 7; Std. 8 & 9 form groups within their own standard. Only one registration per group (one member submits).
Projects can be working/non-working models or scientific demonstrations. They should be creative, safe, school-appropriate, portable, and mess-free. No hazardous materials.
Prepare a short presentation (2–3 minutes) explaining concept, working and application. Two students present at a time while two support. Be ready for judges' questions.
Fill details carefully: Group Leader, Members, Class & Division, Contact number, Project Title, and Description. Last date to submit: 7th Nov 2025. Shortlisted projects will be displayed at the exhibition.
Teacher in-charge: Mrs. Swati Prabhu & Mrs. Milan Karpe.

“Think beyond limits — science begins where curiosity never ends!” ✨

Rules & Guidelines

1. Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based working or non-working models.
2. During the exhibition, 2 students will explain for 2 hours while the other 2 take a break. Teams will alternate roles to ensure active participation.
3. Participants must have complete knowledge of their exhibit. Only well-prepared models and presenters will be considered.
4. Elimination Round: After Diwali Vacation (3rd week of November).
5. Enrollment: Register by 7th November 2025 using the form below.
