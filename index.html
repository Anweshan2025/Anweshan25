About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan — where imagination meets innovation. Here, science isn’t just about formulas and facts — it’s about bringing imagination to life.

We give you the chance to express your talents, whether through creative art, scientific modeling, or innovative demonstrations.

Whether you’re building, experimenting, or dreaming up something entirely new — this is your stage. Sign up for Anweshan 2025 now. It’s never too late for science!

Rules & Guidelines

1. Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based working or non-working models.
2. Participants must be confident in explaining their projects.
3. All materials must be safe and eco-friendly.
4. Deadline: 7th November 2025.
5. Elimination Round: 3rd week of November.
