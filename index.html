About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan — where imagination meets innovation.

Our Annual Science Exhibition – Anweshan 2025 – is an exciting platform for you to showcase your curiosity, creativity, and understanding of scientific concepts. Let your imagination take shape through models, demonstrations, and experiments that reflect the true spirit of science.

Please read the instructions carefully before filling out the Google Form. Each group must consist of 4 students. Mixed groups allowed for Std. 6 & 7, and same standard groups for Std. 8 & 9. Only one registration per group.

Projects can be working/non-working models or scientific demonstrations. They should be creative, safe, and school-appropriate. Models should be portable and mess-free.

Prepare a short presentation (2–3 minutes) explaining your project’s concept, working, and application. Be ready to answer judges’ questions. Two students present at a time while the other two support.

Fill in all details carefully in the form: Group Leader, Members, Class & Division, Contact number, Project Title, and Description. Last date: 7th November 2025.

Teacher In-charge: Mrs. Swati Prabhu & Mrs. Milan Karpe

“Think beyond limits, for science begins where curiosity never ends!” ✨

Rules & Guidelines

1. Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based working or non-working models.
2. During the exhibition, 2 students will explain for 2 hours while the other 2 take a break. They will alternate roles to ensure active participation.
3. Participants must have complete knowledge of their exhibit. Only well-prepared models will be considered.
4. Elimination Round: After Diwali Vacation (3rd week of November).
5. Enrollment: Scroll down below to register by 7th November 2025.
