About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan — where imagination meets innovation. Science here isn’t just about facts — it’s about making your imagination real.
Each group must consist of 4 students (mixed for Std. 6–7, same standard for 8–9). Only one registration per group.
Projects may be working or non-working models. Use safe, eco-friendly materials, and focus on creativity and problem-solving.
Prepare a 2–3 minute explanation for your model and be ready for questions. Two students present while two support.
Fill the form carefully. Last date to register: 7th November 2025. Elimination after Diwali Vacation.

Rules & Guidelines

1. Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based working or non-working models.
2. During the exhibition, 2 students will explain for 2 hours while the other 2 take a break.
3. Participants must have complete knowledge of their exhibit.
4. Elimination Round: After Diwali Vacation (3rd week of November).
5. Enrollment: Register by 7th November 2025 below.
